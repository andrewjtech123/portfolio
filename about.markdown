# Hi there, I’m Andrew Johnston!
Crafting bridges between technology and creativity, I am a seasoned technical writer and leader with a flair for innovation. From pioneering processes to nurturing talent, I facilitate seamless collaboration and drive organizations towards their strategic goals. With a diverse background spanning e-commerce to music production, I bring a unique blend of expertise and empathy to every endeavor.

Currently, I'm a Senior Technical Editor at Blackberry QNX in Ottawa, based out of Aylmer and Wakefield, Quebec. I'm a Shopify Alum who worked at the company for a little over five years helping to build the API documentation team.  

I got my start in technical communications as employee number five of a Montreal tech startup; I helped to grow the company as I wore many hats over a ten year plus tenure. The experience taught me a lot about startup culture and business in general, and it prepared me well for the rest of my career.

In 2017 I was invited to give my talk "Documenting GraphQL at Shopify" at API the Docs in London, England. I was lucky enough to be invited back in 2018 to give another talk called "The Trials and Tribulations of the API style guide". In 2018 I worked with a team of co-organizers at Shopify to reboot the a Write the Docs Ottawa meetup at Shopify. I continued Write the Docs as a virtual meetup over Zoom in 2020 and the event grew to accommodate international speakers and participants.

While I was at Shopify, I had the privilege of bringing Laura Escude to Shopify HQ to give her talk “The Art of Designing a Live Show”. This experience showed me how I could bring my tech career together with my music career.

In addition to producing, I am also a songwriter and I write songs for sync in television and film. My music has been featured in major networks and in films such as “In Your Eyes” produced by Joss Wheedon’s Bellwether pictures.
